My Parents



They are the individuals I cherish the most. My existence in this world is incomplete without them. I express my gratitude towards you, dear mother and father, for being amazing parents. My beloved parents, I hold both of you in high regard and am grateful for your dedication and affection in raising me and my siblings to be a better person. I consider myself fortunate to have you as my parents and thanks to Allah for blessing me with you.

My Siblings



These are my two siblings. Despite the occasional quarrels that are typical of siblings, I cannot imagine my life without them. The affection that sisters have for their younger sisters is irreplaceable, and I am grateful to have been blessed with two siblings who care for me and provide a listening ear for my troubles.
